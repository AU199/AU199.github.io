WHAT IS THE PROBLEM
In 2016, to prevent the negative effects of climate change, the Paris Agreement agreed upon the goal of attaining a temperature only 1.5 degrees Celsius above pre-industrial times (1850-1900) by 2050. However, based on current advancements, the world is far off from this goal: it is projected to reach 1.7, even 1.8, Celsius by 2050. To avoid catastrophe and extensive damage, a solution must be found quickly.
